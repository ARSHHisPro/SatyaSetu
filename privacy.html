Safety Charter

Scam Prevention & Privacy Protection Guidelines

🛡️ 1. Zero Credential Harvesting & Phishing Policy

SatyaSetu operates under a strict anti-phishing regime. We explicitly prohibit the collection of passwords, account numbers, government identifiers, or biometric profiles. This form will never ask for any of those things.

💾 2. Where Your Report Goes

When this app is connected to its project database (Cloud Firestore), your submission is saved securely so the whole team/class can review the shared dashboard. If that connection is unavailable, the report is saved only on your device as a fallback.

🔍 3. Shared Database, No Personal Data

The shared database only ever receives the fields shown on the submission form: date, title, description, classification, severity, and optional media. Access is governed by standard firestore protection rules.

Summary of Privacy Enforcement Controls:

  • PII Audit Shield: Local validation triggers regular expression checks blocking credit cards, phones, emails, or Indian Aadhaar/PAN cards.
  • Local Image Compression: Images scaled to maximum 1000px using client canvas rendering.
  • Lightweight Media Limits: Max file limits are 4MB (images) and 15MB (videos).
