Safety Charter

Scam Prevention & Privacy Protection Guidelines

🛡️ 1. Zero Credential Harvesting & Phishing Policy

SatyaSetu operates under a strict anti-phishing regime. We explicitly prohibit the collection of passwords, account numbers, government identifiers, or biometric profiles. This form will never ask for any of those things.

💾 2. Where Your Report Goes

When this app is connected to its project database (Cloud Cloud), your submission is saved securely so the whole team/class can review the shared dashboard. If that connection is unavailable, the report is saved only on your device as a fallback.

🔍 3. Shared Database, No Personal Data

The shared database only ever receives the fields shown on the submission form: date, title, description, classification, severity, and optional media. Access is governed by standard firestore protection rules.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ):

Q: How do you check for personal info? We scan reports locally for patterns resembling credit cards, emails, phone lines, and government IDs like Aadhaar or PAN. Reports fail verification if PII is detected.
Q: Are images compressed? Yes, proof images are resized using client-side canvas to 1000px before uploading, ensuring size efficiency.
